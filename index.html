Philippines

The Philippines consists of 7,641 islands and is the home of many Pilipinos. It has many attractive sites like Boracay Beach, Rice Terraces, Chocolate Hills, and many more which people around the world visit every year. The Philippines is also a Christian country. You can smell the air of Christmass starting the month of September. Pilipinos, the denizens of this country are generally kind, hardworking, and hospitable. Almost everyone loves to accept guests in their house and would even have a feast, especially if it is a relative who has not visited for a long time.
